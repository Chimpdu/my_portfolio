About Me

Something about me:

BIO

A bachelor student in Software and Systems Engineering at LUT University, Lahti campus. enthusiastic in coding, especially web development

Tangshan, China

Childhood

Born and raised in the vibrant city of Tangshan, China, I spent the first 18 years of my life immersing myself in the rich culture and dynamic atmosphere of my hometown. The foundation of my academic journey was laid at Tangshan No.1 High School, a prestigious institution in the region. In those formative years, my passion for knowledge thrived, particularly in subjects like biology, geography, and English. I was honored to be recognized as one of the top students, a testament to my dedication to academic excellence.

Tianjin, China

College experience in China

My pursuit of knowledge led me to Hebei University of Technology in Tianjin, where I delved into the world of computer science. Over the course of a year, I found myself captivated by the intricate art of coding. The allure of software development took hold of me, and a sincere desire to contribute to the ever-evolving tech industry blossomed within. This transformative period laid the groundwork for my future endeavors, fueling my ambition to make a meaningful impact through my work in the software realm.

Lahti, Finland

Meet Finland

Taking a leap across continents, I embarked on a new chapter of my academic journey in Lahti, Finland. In 2022, I began my bachelor's studies in the Software and Systems Engineering program at Lappeenranta-Lahti University of Technology (LUT), Lahti campus. Here, my fascination with web development reached new heights. Whether navigating the intricacies of frontend design or diving into the complexities of backend development, my enthusiasm for crafting digital experiences grew exponentially. As I navigate this exciting chapter of my life, my ultimate aspiration is to carve a niche for myself in the world of web development, contributing my skills and passion to shape the digital landscape of tomorrow.In tandem with my academic pursuits, I ventured into the realm of part-time work, taking on roles such as a teaching assistant in statistics. I also participated in Lahti Venture Program and Junction 2023. These experiences not only enhanced my practical understanding of my academic field but also fostered valuable interpersonal and communication skills.
